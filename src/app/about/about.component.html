About The App

Hello, I'm Doyin Olarewaju Full stack web dev with a passion for javascript and I made this app.

I've been slacking on doing my TVC for a while now, been postponing without no particular reason and my latest excuse has been that i don't even know where to do it. I asked around and realized that a lot of people where having thesame issue around me. So i decided to make this small utility to help people find locations around where they can do the registration for their voters card.

But a job of this magnitude needs some help. I cannot add all the locations alone. If everybody can just take a moment to add one or two locations around them, we will have all the locations on record in no time.

The app is open source and PR's are welcome. You can also use as you wish, but kindly reference me. A few good words will go a long way to lift of the spirit.
